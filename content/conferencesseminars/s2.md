On December 13, 2023, I had the opportunity to present a research paper co-authored with Marlène Guillon on the prevalence and factors associated with the adoption of heat-adapted behaviors among residents of a region in the south of France at the 45th conference of French health economists (JESF) held in Bordeaux.
